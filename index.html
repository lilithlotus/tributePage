Bill Nye: The Science Guy

American Science Educator, Activist, Television Presenter, and Mechanical Engineer

Basic Information:

  • Full Name: William Sanford Nye
  • Date of Birth: November 27, 1955
  • Birthplace: Washington D.C.

Education:

  • Sidwell Friends School, Washington D.C.
  • Cornell University, Ithaca, NY- Graduated in 1977 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering

Noteworthy Achievements:

  • Worked for Boeing and Sunstrand Data Corporation in Seattle, Washington
  • Invented a hydraulic resonance suppressor tube used on 747 airplanes
  • Host of Bill Nye: The Science Guy educational program which was nominated for 23 Emmy Awards, of which it won 19
  • CEO of the Planetary Society
  • Multiple time Best Selling Author
  • Has appeared both on television and in movies, providing cameos and educational segments
  • Holds several US Patents
  • Fellow of the Committe for Skeptical Inquiry
